Located in Newport, NC, near Morehead City on the outer banks, this secluded US Forest Service campground provides excellent tent camping in the Croatan National Forest near the beach.

This campground is operated by the US Forest Service year-round.

Campground Information

Ambience
Campsites are nestled under tall loblolly pines next to the Bogue Sound.
The campground is rarely filled to capacity and generally has light usage.
Number of Camp Sites
15
Cost
$8 per night
Hours
Open year-round; the gate is closed from 10pm-8am but can be opened by the campground host for emergencies
Restrictions
No alcohol; dogs must be kept on a leash
Reservations
Not available and generally not necessary - first come, first serve
Fires
Fire rings at each site with wood is on site for fires
Amenities
Vault toilets, picnic tables, fire grills, parking spurs, and tent pads at every site
Staff
A campground host is on site at all times
RV Hookups
RVs up to 50 feet are welcome, but no electrical hookups or other RV amenities are available
